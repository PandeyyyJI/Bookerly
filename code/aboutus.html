ABOUT US

Welcome To Bookerly!

Bookerly helps both the teacher and students in this era of online education. It contains all the study materials at one place along with a TRL feature to maintain a list. Bookerly helps the users to find appropriate materials from the internet all at one place.

Convenience includes the overall ease of finding useful content, time spent on looking for a particular content, minimization of overall surfing effort.

Added to it Bookerly allows the users to read at the own convenience, and to save the burden of carrying books around while traveling time and spend their valuable boot space with important articles.

Researchers identify convenience as a ‘fundamental objective’ related to online reading . In addition to ease of finding products here and surfing time reduction, consumers can read without time limitation with 24-hr access at their convenience.
