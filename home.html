Welcome to Bookerly

Hello visitor!! welcome to our website. We created this website to help both the teacher and students in this era of online education. Our website contains all the study materials at one place along with a TRL feature to maintain a to-read/watch list, or organize your day. This system is designed to help the users find appropriate materials from the internet at one place.The system will be run on a central server with each user having a remote user interface through a web browser to interact with it.The system will allow customers to browse, search, select, and add books to a to-read list.The website will provide book recommendation on the basis of the user’s previous viewed content.The customer will be provided full security system. Anyone can use this website ranging from a child to an old age person.Users can also share their reviews and write comments on the books.Convenience includes the overall ease of finding a useful content, time spent on looking for a particular content, minimization of overall surfing effort . Online library allows the users to read at the own convenience, and to save the burden of carrying books around while traveling time and spend their valuable boot space with important articles. Researchers identify convenience as a ‘fundamental objective’ related to online reading . In addition to ease of finding products here and surfing time reduction, consumers can read without time limitation with 24-hr access at their convenience because the World Wide Web never closes. We hope you will like our webpage.
